Among Us Jupyter Simulator

Using Python in Jupyter Notebook, I am creating a text-to-action simulation of the game, Among Us.

To the left is a small screenshot of the beginning sequence
and a few commands you can input (They have not been completed yet)

NPCs and Characters

In this version of the simulation, you are always the red imposter, among 6 crewmates.

Actions

You can take multiple actions similar to regular Among Us in this simulation, such as killing, venting, sabotaging, and of course, moving around.

NPC Behavoir

NPC will be controlled by a RNG, selecting randomized values as actions to do tasks, move around, etc.
Obviously, they will run to call an emergency meeting if they see you vent or kill a fellow crewmate.

Action-Based Outcomes

Instead of having outcomes of votes being based on RNG, it is instead based on a variety of actual factors.
These include your proximity to dead bodies, if you faked tasks, and NPCs witnessing you at different locations.

Future Ideas

After the main functionality of the game is set up, more crewmates will be implemented, along with possibly a NPC fellow imposter, and maybe a choice on game size, map, and roles!
